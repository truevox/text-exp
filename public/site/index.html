  1. Install the Extension: Download PuffPuffPaste from the Chrome Web Store (coming soon!).
  2. Open Options Page: Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar and select "Options" or "Settings".
  3. Configure Storage: On the Options page, choose your preferred storage method:
    • Local Folder Sync: Select a folder on your computer to store snippets.
    • Cloud Sync: Connect to Google Drive, Dropbox, or OneDrive and select your snippet folders.
  4. Create Snippets: Use the "Add Snippet" button in the extension popup or Options page to define your triggers and content.
  5. Start Expanding! In any text field, type your defined trigger (e.g., ;hello) followed by a space or tab, and watch your words expand!
